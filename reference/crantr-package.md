# crantr: R Client Access to Clonal Raider ANT (CRANT) Datasets

Access to the Clonal Raider ANT (CRANT) EM datasets, starting with
CRANTb and focussing on support for autosegmentation. Organised as a
wrapper around the fafbseg and bancr packages.
